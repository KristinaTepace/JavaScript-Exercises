MY FAIVORITE MOVIES

TITANIC

James Cameron's "Titanic" is an epic, action-packed romance set against the ill-fated maiden voyage of the R.M.S. Titanic; the pride and joy of the White Star Line and, at the time, the largest moving object ever built. She was the most luxurious liner of her era -- the "ship of dreams" -- which ultimately carried over 1,500 people to their death in the ice cold waters of the North Atlantic in the early hours of April 15, 1912.

SAW

The Saw franchise's villain was inspired by a serial tickler. Wan revealed that he was inspired by a real-life criminal case he heard about when he was 17. The case apparently involved a serial offender who broke into people's houses to tickle their children's feet, which made an impact on the "Saw" filmmaker.

STEP-UP

Step Up is an American romantic dance film multi-media franchise created by Duane Adler for the first film in the series, Step Up. The franchise includes six films and a television series. The series has received generally mixed reviews from critics. It has grossed a collective total of $651 million.
